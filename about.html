About Us

Glamour Touch Salon was founded by Ms. Esther Namutebi, a professional beautician with over 15 years of experience in the hair and beauty industry. Her vision was to create a salon that blends African beauty traditions with modern international standards.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire confidence and self-love by providing quality, affordable, and relaxing beauty experiences. We believe that beauty is not a luxury — it’s a way to express self-care and individuality.

Our Vision

To be Kampala’s most trusted and innovative beauty destination, recognized for exceptional service, professionalism, and community impact.

Our Core Values

  • Professionalism: We treat every client with respect, honesty, and courtesy.
  • Cleanliness: We maintain strict hygiene and use sterilized tools at all times.
  • Creativity: We stay updated with the latest beauty trends and styles.
  • Customer Care: We go the extra mile to make every visit enjoyable.
