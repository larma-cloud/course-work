Welcome to Eddyi's Unisex Salon

Welcome to Glamour Touch Salon, your number one beauty destination located along Kampala Road, opposite Garden City Mall. Since our establishment in 2015, we have grown to become one of Kampala’s most trusted beauty and hair care centers. We believe every individual deserves to look and feel beautiful — and that’s what we deliver every single day.

Our salon combines creativity, professionalism, and relaxation. Whether you want a quick haircut, a luxury full-body massage, bridal makeup, or nail art, our expert stylists and beauty therapists ensure you leave with confidence and satisfaction.

Our Promise to You

At Glamour Touch, we promise to use only high-quality products, maintain a clean and peaceful environment, and treat every customer with warmth and respect. We value your comfort, and every service is tailored to meet your unique beauty needs.
